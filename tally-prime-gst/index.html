Complete Tally Prime with GST — accounting, billing, inventory, payroll, TDS, and real-time P&L, balance sheet and GSTR reports.

The reports module covers real-time GSTR-1, GSTR-2, GSTR-3B, Trading & P&L, Trial Balance and Balance Sheet generation directly in Tally Prime.
