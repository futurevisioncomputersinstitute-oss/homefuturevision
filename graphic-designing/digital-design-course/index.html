Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need a design background?

No. This course starts from zero and builds professional skills systematically. Many students have never used design software before enrolling.

What software do I need?

Adobe Creative Cloud (student pricing available) and Figma (free plan). You’ll need a laptop with at least 8GB RAM. All tools are taught from the beginning.

How long are classes each week?

3 sessions per week, 3 hours each, at our Surat centres. Classes are kept small — max 20 students — for focused, personalised attention.

What do I graduate with?

A portfolio of 10–12 production-ready marketing design projects plus a complete brand kit — ready to show employers or clients.

Is placement assistance provided?

Yes. Resume curation, portfolio review, interview preparation and placement guidance are all included in the programme.
