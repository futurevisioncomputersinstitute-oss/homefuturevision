Course Structure

What You'll Learn

01
Weeks 1–8

Design Foundations

Visual hierarchy, typography, colour theory, grid systems, Photoshop and Illustrator fundamentals. You'll design real ad banners, social posts and print materials from day one.

02
Weeks 9–20

Brand & Campaign Design

Logo design, brand guidelines, multi-channel campaign creation, InDesign for print, email template design and corporate stationery suites.

03
Weeks 21–32

Social media content systems, animated graphics in After Effects, video editing in Premiere Pro, motion templates for Reels and YouTube.

04
Weeks 33–44

Portfolio & Career Prep

Portfolio curation, personal brand website, resume and LinkedIn makeover, client brief simulations and final presentation to industry mentors.
