Course Curriculum

11 Months. 6 Phases.

01

Design Foundations

Months 1–2 · Adobe Photoshop · Adobe Illustrator

Start with the two most essential tools in professional design. Learn Photoshop for photo editing, compositing and retouching. Learn Illustrator for logos, vector graphics and brand assets. You’ll complete real briefs from week one — ad banners, social posts and posters. By the end of Phase 1, you can handle any visual production task a marketing team throws at you.

  • ✓Photoshop: selections, masking, layers, retouching, compositing
  • ✓Illustrator: pen tool, shapes, logo construction, icon design
  • ✓Typography, colour theory, visual hierarchy, grid systems
  • ✓Projects: social media post, event poster, product label
02

Brand & Marketing Design

Months 3–4 · Canva Pro · Adobe Express

Build brand identities and marketing collateral at professional speed. Canva and Adobe Express are not shortcuts — in marketing teams they are primary tools. You’ll learn to customise templates beyond recognition, build brand kits, create email campaign graphics, pitch decks and social media content systems at scale.

  • ✓Canva Pro: brand kits, team templates, batch design
  • ✓Adobe Express: social graphics, flyers, quick campaign assets
  • ✓Brand identity: logo system, colour palette, typography hierarchy
  • ✓Projects: brand kit, email template series, social content calendar
03

Web & UI Design for Marketing

Months 5–6 · Figma

Every marketing professional needs to design for screens. Figma is the industry standard for web and UI design. You’ll design landing pages, hero sections, email layouts and ad creatives that hand off cleanly to developers. No coding required — you’ll learn to think in grids, components and design systems the way product and marketing teams work.

  • ✓Figma: frames, auto-layout, components, variants, prototyping
  • ✓Landing page design, web banner ad systems, mobile layouts
  • ✓Responsive design principles, design handoff to developers
  • ✓Projects: landing page, mobile screen design, display ad set
04

AI-Powered Design Workflows

Months 7–8 · Adobe Firefly · Midjourney · Ideogram · Canva AI · ChatGPT · Claude · Gemini

AI is not replacing designers — it is replacing designers who don’t use AI. This phase teaches you to generate, edit and direct AI visuals across every major platform. You’ll use language models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to write briefs and generate copy, and image AI (Firefly, Midjourney, Ideogram) to create visuals you refine in Photoshop and Illustrator.

  • ✓Adobe Firefly: generative fill, text-to-image, style transfer inside Photoshop
  • ✓Midjourney & Ideogram: prompt engineering for marketing visuals
  • ✓ChatGPT, Claude & Gemini: brief writing, copy, design ideation
  • ✓Canva AI: Magic Write, Dream Lab, background removal at scale
  • ✓Projects: AI-assisted campaign, 10 social posts using AI + Photoshop
05

Video Editing & AI Video

Months 9–10 · Adobe Premiere Pro · Runway · Google Veo

Video is the dominant format in digital marketing. You’ll learn professional editing in Premiere Pro — cuts, colour grading, captions, transitions — then layer in AI video generation with Runway and Google Veo to create content that used to require a full production team. Reels, YouTube ads, brand films and product videos all covered.

  • ✓Premiere Pro: timeline editing, colour grading, captions, export for social
  • ✓Runway: text-to-video, image-to-video, AI video effects
  • ✓Google Veo: prompt-driven cinematic video generation
  • ✓Projects: 30-second brand video, AI-generated product Reel
06

Portfolio & Career Launch

Month 11 · All Tools

Your final month is dedicated to building a portfolio that gets you hired. Curate 10–12 projects into a professional case-study portfolio, set up your Behance or personal website, refine your CV and LinkedIn, and present your best work to a panel of industry mentors for live feedback. Placement guidance, mock interviews and client brief simulations included.

  • ✓Portfolio curation: 10–12 real-world projects across all 5 phases
  • ✓Behance profile + personal portfolio website setup
  • ✓CV, LinkedIn & personal brand makeover
  • ✓Mock interviews, client brief simulations, mentor presentation

Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need a design background?

No. This course starts from zero and builds professional skills systematically. Many students have never used design software before enrolling.

What software do I need?

Adobe Creative Cloud (student pricing available) and Figma (free plan). You’ll need a laptop with at least 8GB RAM. All tools are taught from the beginning.

How long are classes each week?

3 sessions per week, 3 hours each, at our Surat centres. Classes are kept small — max 20 students — for focused, personalised attention.

What do I graduate with?

A portfolio of 10–12 production-ready marketing design projects plus a complete brand kit — ready to show employers or clients.

Is placement assistance provided?

Yes. Resume curation, portfolio review, interview preparation and placement guidance are all included in the programme.
