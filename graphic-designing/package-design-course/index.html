Course Curriculum

6 Months. 6 Phases.

01

Brand Fundamentals & Vector Design

Month 1 · Adobe Illustrator · ChatGPT · Claude · Gemini · Perplexity · NotebookLM

Every great package starts with a brand that is understood. You’ll master Adobe Illustrator from scratch — vector drawing, the Pen tool, bezier curves, typography, logo construction and colour theory for print. In parallel, AI tools become your research engine: Perplexity and NotebookLM for competitive market analysis, ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini for writing brand strategy briefs, naming concepts and target audience profiles that inform every design decision.

  • ✓Illustrator: vector fundamentals, Pen tool, Pathfinder, type, colour systems
  • ✓Logo and wordmark construction for packaging identity
  • ✓Perplexity & NotebookLM: market research, competitor shelf analysis
  • ✓ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini: brand brief writing, naming, audience personas
  • ✓Print colour: CMYK, Pantone, spot colour, ink trapping basics
  • ✓Deliverables: brand identity kit, colour palette, typography system
02

Packaging Structure & Dieline Design

Month 2 · Adobe Illustrator · Canva · ChatGPT · Claude

Packaging is a 3D problem solved on a flat surface. You’ll learn structural design — how boxes, sleeves, pouches, tubes and labels are engineered — and build technical dielines in Illustrator with bleed, safe zones and fold marks. Canva handles rapid concept layouts for label design and social mockups. ChatGPT and Claude help generate copy frameworks, regulatory text blocks and ingredient list structures so the design and copy always work together.

  • ✓Dieline construction: boxes, cartons, sleeves, pouches, bottle labels
  • ✓Illustrator: bleed, safe zones, fold/cut lines, layer organisation for print
  • ✓Canva: quick label layouts, digital mockups, social media packaging shots
  • ✓ChatGPT & Claude: packaging copy, ingredient lists, regulatory text, nutritional claims
  • ✓Prepress: print-ready file preparation, preflight checklist
  • ✓Deliverables: 3 complete dielines with all print panels designed
03

Product Photography & Image Retouching

Month 3 · Adobe Photoshop · Photoshop AI · Adobe Firefly

Premium packaging demands premium imagery. You’ll learn Adobe Photoshop inside out for product retouching — clipping paths, colour correction, light manipulation and surface texture work. Photoshop AI’s generative expand and object removal tools transform complex retouching tasks into seconds. Adobe Firefly generates photorealistic backgrounds, surface textures and abstract compositions that would take hours to shoot or source, all fully commercial-use safe.

  • ✓Photoshop: layer masking, clipping paths, adjustment layers, smart objects
  • ✓Product retouching: colour correction, glare removal, surface cleaning
  • ✓Photoshop AI: generative expand, object removal, background replacement
  • ✓Adobe Firefly: text-to-image backgrounds, texture generation, atmospheric scenes
  • ✓E-commerce packaging shots: white background, lifestyle, detail crops
  • ✓Deliverables: full retouched image set for 2 packaging projects
04

3D Packaging & Photorealistic Mockups

Month 4 · Adobe Dimension · Blender · Midjourney · Recraft

Clients and buyers need to see a product before it exists. You’ll use Adobe Dimension to wrap your flat artwork onto 3D bottle, box and pouch models and render photorealistic presentations in minutes. Blender takes this further with custom 3D geometry, lighting rigs and camera angles for hero shots. Midjourney and Recraft generate stunning concept imagery and lifestyle scenes that you composite with your 3D renders to produce presentation decks that win client approval.

  • ✓Adobe Dimension: material placement, label wrapping, lighting, rendering
  • ✓Blender: 3D modelling for custom packaging shapes, HDRI lighting, Cycles render
  • ✓Midjourney: concept art, lifestyle imagery, atmospheric backgrounds
  • ✓Recraft: vector illustrations, brand icons, label artwork at production quality
  • ✓Client presentation decks: mockup compositions, shelf renders, hero shots
  • ✓Deliverables: photorealistic 3D renders for all project packaging
05

AI-First Design Workflows

Month 5 · Midjourney · Ideogram · Recraft · Canva AI · Adobe Firefly · ChatGPT · Claude · Gemini

This phase rewires how you design. You’ll build complete AI-first workflows where every creative decision is accelerated by AI generation and human refinement. Ideogram produces typographic label concepts with legible text. Midjourney and Recraft generate illustration-led packaging artwork. Canva AI produces variants at speed. The real skill is prompt engineering, directing AI output, then refining in Illustrator and Photoshop to production standard. You’ll complete a full product line using nothing but AI-first methods.

  • ✓Ideogram: text-accurate AI label concepts, typography-led packaging visuals
  • ✓Midjourney: illustration-led label art, premium lifestyle imagery
  • ✓Recraft: scalable vector illustrations, icons, pattern systems for labels
  • ✓Canva AI + Firefly: rapid design variations, A/B test concepts, social assets
  • ✓ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini: naming, taglines, pitch decks, client email scripts
  • ✓Deliverables: complete product line (3 SKUs) designed with AI-first workflow
06

Portfolio, Case Studies & Career Launch

Month 6 · All Tools · Claude · ChatGPT · Canva

Your portfolio is your pitch book to every brand and agency. This final phase is dedicated to curating 4–6 complete packaging projects — each presented as a professional case study from brief through production-ready files. Claude and ChatGPT sharpen your case study writing and pitch copy. Canva builds the presentation decks. You leave with a Behance profile, a PDF portfolio, a Freelancing starter kit and mentor sign-off ready for the job market.

  • ✓4–6 complete packaging case studies: brief, research, design, 3D, final files
  • ✓Behance and portfolio PDF presentation build
  • ✓Claude & ChatGPT: case study writing, client proposal templates, pitch scripts
  • ✓Freelancing primer: pricing, client briefs, revision management, contracts
  • ✓Mock client presentations and mentor critique sessions
  • ✓Placement guidance: resume, LinkedIn, agency and brand outreach

Common Questions

FAQs

Is this course for beginners?

Basic Illustrator familiarity helps but is not required. All tools are taught from the level needed for packaging design work. Many students start with minimal software experience.

Do I need a 3D background?

No. Adobe Dimension and Blender are taught from scratch within the course. You’ll produce professional-quality 3D renders by Phase 3 regardless of prior experience.

What industries hire packaging designers?

FMCG, pharma, cosmetics, food and beverage, e-commerce, retail and luxury goods are all major employers. Packaging is one of the most consistently in-demand design specialisms.

Will I learn sustainable packaging?

Yes. Phase 1 covers materials, sustainability principles and eco-friendly structural choices — increasingly important criteria for brand clients and agencies.

What does my final portfolio include?

A minimum of 6 complete packaging projects: labels, primary packs, secondary packs, retail display and a full 3D-rendered brand packaging system across a product line.
