Course Structure

What You'll Learn

01
Weeks 1–6

Foundations of Packaging

History of packaging, structural types, materials and sustainability, dieline anatomy, Illustrator for packaging and print production basics.

02
Weeks 7–14

Brand-Led Packaging Design

Label design, primary and secondary packaging, typography for packaging, colour management and CMYK print prep.

03
Weeks 15–20

3D Visualisation & Mockups

Adobe Dimension, Blender basics for product renders, Keyshot fundamentals, photorealistic mockup creation and presentation decks.

04
Weeks 21–24

Retail, Portfolio & Career

Retail display design, POS materials, packaging system design across product lines, portfolio curation and industry presentations.

Common Questions

FAQ

Is this suitable for beginners?
Some basic Illustrator familiarity helps but is not required. We cover the tools from the basics needed for packaging.
Do I need a 3D software background?
No. Adobe Dimension and Blender are taught from scratch within the course context.
What industries hire packaging designers?
FMCG, pharma, cosmetics, food and beverage, e-commerce, retail and luxury goods — all are major employers.
Will I learn sustainable packaging?
Yes. Phase 1 covers materials, sustainability principles and eco-friendly structural choices.
What does the portfolio include?
A minimum of 6 complete packaging projects: labels, primary packs, secondary packs, retail display and a full 3D-rendered brand packaging system.
