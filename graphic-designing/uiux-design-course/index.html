Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need coding skills?
No. This is a design course. Understanding basic HTML/CSS is a bonus but not required.
Is 5 months enough?
Yes — the curriculum is intensive and project-led. You'll graduate with 4–6 full UX case studies.
What is the difference between UX and UI?
UX covers research, structure and logic. UI covers visual design and interaction. This course teaches both.
Will I work on real briefs?
Yes. Every phase involves live industry-style briefs across fintech, healthcare, e-commerce and SaaS.
Can I get placed after this course?
We provide portfolio reviews, interview prep and guidance. Placements are not guaranteed but our support is structured and active.
