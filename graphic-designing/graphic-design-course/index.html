Course Curriculum

11 Months. 7 Phases.

01

Print Design Foundations

Months 1–2 · Adobe Photoshop · Adobe Illustrator · Firefly · Midjourney · Ideogram · ChatGPT · Claude

The fundamentals never go out of style. You’ll master Photoshop for photo retouching, compositing and image manipulation, and Illustrator for vector drawing, logo construction, typography and print-ready artwork. AI accelerates ideation from day one: Midjourney and Ideogram generate concept visuals, Firefly creates commercial-use imagery and textures, and ChatGPT with Claude help write creative briefs and client communication. Every print deliverable — poster, flyer, brochure, logo — is built to professional production standard.

  • ✓Photoshop: masking, retouching, compositing, smart objects, adjustment layers
  • ✓Illustrator: vector paths, logo design, typography, Pantone colour, print setup
  • ✓Print production: bleed, safe zones, CMYK, prepress and file preparation
  • ✓Midjourney & Ideogram: concept generation, moodboards, reference imagery
  • ✓Firefly: generative backgrounds, textures, object removal and expand
  • ✓Deliverables: brand identity kit, poster series, brochure, print-ready files
02

Digital Design & Social Media Content

Months 3–4 · Canva · Edits · Canva AI · ChatGPT · Claude · Gemini

Speed and volume define social media design. You’ll learn Canva at a professional level — brand kits, shared templates, content calendars and multi-format exports for Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn and beyond. Edits gives you mobile-first short-form video creation to match the Reels and Shorts era. Canva AI generates copy and layout variations instantly. ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini write captions, content strategies and campaign briefs that transform you from a visual creator into a full content production unit.

  • ✓Canva: brand kits, templates, multi-format social exports, presentation design
  • ✓Edits: short-form video creation, Reels/Shorts editing, transitions, text overlays
  • ✓Canva AI: Magic Design, AI image generation, copy writing inside Canva
  • ✓ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini: captions, hashtag strategy, content calendar, brand copy
  • ✓Platform-specific design: Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, WhatsApp Business
  • ✓Deliverables: 30-day content calendar with designed assets across 3 platforms
03

Motion Design & Video Production

Months 5–6 · After Effects · Premiere Pro · Runway · Google Veo · ElevenLabs

Motion is the most in-demand skill in graphic design today. You’ll learn After Effects for kinetic typography, logo animations, motion graphics and broadcast-quality title sequences. Premiere Pro handles full video production and colour grading. Then AI takes over: Runway and Google Veo generate video from text prompts and images — entire ad sequences without a camera. ElevenLabs creates studio-quality AI voiceovers in seconds. You’ll produce complete video ads, social reels and animated brand assets.

  • ✓After Effects: keyframe animation, motion paths, kinetic typography, expressions
  • ✓Premiere Pro: video editing, colour grading, audio sync, export for all platforms
  • ✓Logo animation, lower thirds, motion graphics templates, brand intros
  • ✓Runway & Google Veo: text-to-video, image-to-video, AI-generated ad scenes
  • ✓ElevenLabs: AI voiceover generation, voice cloning, multilingual narration
  • ✓Deliverables: logo animation, social reel, full 30-second video ad
04

Packaging Design

Month 7 · Adobe Illustrator · Adobe Dimension · Midjourney · Ideogram · Recraft

Packaging design is one of the highest-paid specialisations in graphic design. Building on your Illustrator foundation, you’ll move into structural packaging: dieline construction for boxes, bottles, pouches and labels, then artwork creation and brand system application across multiple SKUs. Adobe Dimension wraps your flat artwork onto 3D forms for photorealistic client presentations. Midjourney, Ideogram and Recraft generate label art and illustration systems at production speed.

  • ✓Structural packaging: dielines for boxes, labels, pouches, sleeves, bottles
  • ✓Print-ready packaging artwork: bleed, die-cut registration, Pantone output
  • ✓Adobe Dimension: 3D mockup rendering, material wrapping, lighting, scene composition
  • ✓Midjourney & Ideogram: label art concepts, typographic packaging visuals
  • ✓Recraft: scalable vector illustrations and icon systems for product labels
  • ✓Deliverables: 3-SKU product line with dielines, artwork and 3D renders
05

Web & Interface Design

Months 8–9 · Figma · FigJam · Framer · Claude · ChatGPT

Graphic designers who can design for screens are irreplaceable. You’ll learn Figma from fundamentals to advanced — components, auto-layout, design systems, responsive grids and interactive prototypes. FigJam handles visual collaboration, user flow mapping and design thinking workshops. Framer turns your Figma designs into live, published websites with animations and CMS — no code. Claude and ChatGPT write UX copy, microcopy and content strategy so design and words always fit together.

  • ✓Figma: frames, components, auto-layout, variables, design systems, prototyping
  • ✓FigJam: user flows, sitemaps, design sprints, collaborative workshops
  • ✓Framer: component animation, CMS, published portfolio and client websites
  • ✓Responsive web design: mobile, tablet, desktop breakpoints
  • ✓Claude & ChatGPT: UX copy, headlines, microcopy, content hierarchy
  • ✓Deliverables: complete UI design system + live Framer website
06

3D Design & CGI Advertising

Month 10 · Blender · Runway · Google Veo · Midjourney

CGI ads have replaced traditional photography for hundreds of brands. You’ll learn Blender for 3D modelling, lighting and rendering — creating product visualisations, CGI brand spots and the kind of hyperreal scenes you see in premium brand campaigns. Runway and Google Veo bring your 3D renders to life as AI-generated video. Midjourney generates reference imagery and atmospheric compositions. You’ll produce a complete CGI ad from concept through final video render.

  • ✓Blender: 3D modelling, materials, HDRI lighting, Cycles rendering, camera work
  • ✓Product visualisation: 360 renders, hero shots, animated turntables
  • ✓CGI brand spots: scene building, fluid simulation, particle effects
  • ✓Runway & Google Veo: render-to-video, AI-generated cinematic transitions
  • ✓Midjourney: concept art, lighting references, storyboard imagery for CGI planning
  • ✓Deliverables: 3D product renders + 15-second CGI brand ad
07

Portfolio, Case Studies & Career Launch

Month 11 · All Tools · Claude · ChatGPT · Canva · Framer

Eleven months of real work becomes an unstoppable portfolio. This final phase is structured around curating your best 6–8 projects into professional case studies — each showing the brief, the process and the outcome. Claude and ChatGPT write your case study copy, bio and cover letters. Your portfolio site is built and published in Framer. You leave with a Behance profile, a live portfolio URL, a freelancing starter kit and mentor sign-off for the job market or your own studio.

  • ✓6–8 portfolio projects across print, digital, motion, packaging, web and 3D
  • ✓Framer portfolio site: live, custom domain, fully designed and published
  • ✓Claude & ChatGPT: case study writing, bio, cover letters, LinkedIn headline
  • ✓Behance profile setup and project presentation
  • ✓Freelancing kit: pricing guide, proposal templates, client communication scripts
  • ✓Mock interviews, portfolio presentations, industry mentor critique

Common Questions

FAQs

Is this course for complete beginners?

Yes. Many students have never opened Photoshop before enrolling. The programme starts from absolute zero and builds systematically to professional-level skills.

How is this different from the Digital Design course?

The Digital Design course focuses on marketing outputs. This course spans the full profession — print, brand identity, motion and digital — all covered in depth over 11 months.

Do I need my own computer?

Yes. A laptop with at least 8GB RAM is recommended. Adobe Creative Cloud (student pricing available) and Figma (free plan) are the primary tools you’ll use.

What does the final portfolio include?

12–18 projects spanning print, brand identity, digital and motion — a full-breadth portfolio that demonstrates professional range to studios, agencies and brands.

Are classes in English or Gujarati?

Classes are taught in a mix of English and Gujarati/Hindi to ensure every student fully grasps concepts, processes and terminology without a language barrier.
