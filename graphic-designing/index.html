Full Curriculum

16 Modules — From Design Fundamentals to Agentic AI

Every module is studio-taught with real projects. Not just theory — every concept is applied to actual design work before you move on.

Module 01
Fundamentals of Design
Contrast, alignment, repetition, hierarchy and balance — plus decoding brand language, combining type & imagery and visual persuasion.
Module 02
Design for Print
Grid systems, layout & page design, type for print, usability and printing techniques. Project: brochures, posters, business cards, flyers & logos.
Module 03
Design for Digital
Designing for social media screens, setting a visual language & concept, and storyboarding. Project: creatives for social media.
Module 04
Brand Identity Design
Brand consistency, visual storytelling and professional presentation. Project: logo design, package design and 3D mockups.
Module 05
Adobe Photoshop
Image editing, selection & masking, layers & compositing, retouching, filters & effects, text and advanced compositing techniques.
Module 06
Adobe Illustrator
Shapes & paths, the pen tool, colours & gradients, colour management, brush tools and advanced typography & text effects.
Module 07
Product & Package Design
Detailed vector designs, 3D models in Adobe Dimension, materials & textures, lighting, camera perspective and realistic mockups.
Module 08
Adobe Lightroom
Importing & organising photos, the library module, basic & advanced editing, presets & profiles, retouching and exporting.
Module 09
Canva
Fast graphic design, social posts & presentations, image editing, typography, collaboration, animation, video and social media marketing.
Module 10
Video Editing — Premiere Pro
Timeline editing, transitions, audio editing, colour correction & grading, titles, multicam and exporting. Project: multicam, short films & social content.
Module 11
Motion Graphics — After Effects
Keyframe animation, masks, compositing, text animation, motion tracking and 3D. Project: character animation, product animation & wedding invitations.
Module 12
3D Design with Blender
Modeling & sculpting, texturing, shading & UV mapping, lighting & rendering, rigging & animation, physics simulations and motion tracking.
Module 13 · 11-Month Track
UI/UX Design with Figma
Wireframing, prototyping, design systems, auto-layout and components in Figma. Design real app & website interfaces.
Module 14 · 11-Month Track
Claude & Agentic AI for Designers
Use Claude and Agentic AI to ideate, generate assets, automate repetitive design tasks and build AI-assisted creative workflows. Unique to Future Vision in Surat.
Module 15 · 11-Month Track
CGI Advertisements
Blender basics, camera tracking, realistic CGI, working with 3D assets, scene optimisation and photoreal product ads with industrial projects.
Module 16
20+ AI Designing Tools
Adobe Firefly, Media.io, AutoDraw, Let’s Enhance, PicWish and 20+ more AI tools woven through the course to design, enhance and produce work faster.
